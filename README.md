# Kedarkantha Trek Guide

The Kedarkantha Trek is one of the most popular winter treks in India. Located in the Uttarkashi district of Uttarakhand, this trek offers breathtaking views of the snow-capped peaks of the Himalayas.

## Trek Details:
- **Duration**: 6 days
- **Maximum Altitude**: 12,500 feet
- **Best Time to Visit**: December to April
- **Difficulty Level**: Moderate to Challenging

## Why Choose Kedarkantha Trek?

- Beautiful scenic views
- Snow-covered landscapes during winter
- Opportunities for camping and star-gazing
- Perfect for beginners and intermediate trekkers

## More Information:
For detailed information on Kedarkantha Trek, visit the [Himalayan Hikers - Kedarkantha Trek](https://himalayanhikers.in/kedarkantha-trek).

## How to Prepare for the Trek:

- **Physical Fitness**: Make sure you are physically prepared for the trek, with some cardio or hiking practice.
- **What to Pack**:
  - Warm clothes (layers are important)
  - Sturdy trekking shoes
  - Sleeping bag (if camping)
  - Water bottle and snacks
  - Trekking poles
- **Guided Tours**: It is recommended to trek with a guide or as part of a group for safety.
